Where Things Break Down

Here's what usually gets in the way.

Your offer isn't doing the work it should

It's hard to explain, hard for people to understand, and even harder to get them excited about it. Conversations stall before they start.

Your content gets views but not clients

There's no bridge between people watching and people reaching out. You're building an audience without a pipeline.

Discovery calls happen by accident, not by design

When a call happens it goes well, but there's no reliable way to get them on the calendar consistently.

Good months feel lucky, not repeatable

You've signed clients before. But you're not sure exactly what worked, so you can't do it again on purpose.

The coaching is good. The system just isn't there yet.

What You'll Build

One system. Three parts.

The Attract, Connect, Convert framework is the core of everything you'll build inside the Accelerator. Each part feeds the next.

Attract

Get the right people in

A clear offer and a visibility system on one primary channel, so the right coaches find you consistently, not occasionally.

Connect

Turn content into conversations

The bridge between someone watching and someone reaching out: lead magnets, calls to action, and outreach that starts real conversations.

Convert

Run a repeatable sales process

A discovery call framework you use every time and can improve, so when a call happens, you know exactly how to move it forward.

How It Works

Build it. Prove it. Run it.

Phase 1 · Months 1–2

Build

  • Clarify your offer and ideal client
  • Set up your primary visibility channel
  • Build the bridge from content to conversation
  • Design your sales conversation framework
  • Book and run your first discovery calls

Goal: one new client from the system, not luck.

Phase 2 · Months 3–4

Repeat

  • Run the full Attract, Connect, Convert loop weekly
  • Identify what's working and what's leaking
  • Fix the weakest link in the system
  • Build a simplified weekly routine you can maintain

This is where most people fall apart. This phase is about consistency.

Phase 3 · Months 5–12

Sustain

  • Monthly group calls and hot seats
  • Async support for questions between calls
  • Quarterly reviews of numbers and goals
  • Optimize, raise rates, add referral systems
  • Stay consistent and hit your full-time income goal

The heavy lifting is done. Now it's about momentum.

FAQ

Common questions.

Who is this program designed for?

The Steady Growth Accelerator is for established coaches and consultants who know their work gets results but can't make client-getting feel consistent. You should have an offer and have sold it before. If you're still in the early stages of building, The Strong Start is a better first step.

What's the difference between The Strong Start and the full Accelerator?

The Strong Start is a 30-day sprint you can join before committing to anything bigger. By Day 30 you'll have a clear offer, a content system live, and at least one discovery call booked from the system. If you love it, your investment applies toward the full Accelerator and you pay only the difference. If it's not the right fit, you walk away with real momentum and zero strings attached.

How does the program work week to week?

In Phases 1 and 2 (Months 1–4) you'll have weekly group coaching calls with hot seats and direct feedback, monthly curriculum drops with lessons and worksheets, a monthly 1:1 touchpoint, and ongoing async support inside the community. In Phase 3 (Months 5–12), calls shift to biweekly and include quarterly reviews of your numbers and goals.

Are sessions recorded?

Yes. All group calls are recorded so you won't miss anything if a session conflicts with your schedule.

How much time do I need to commit each week?

During Phases 1 and 2, plan for a weekly group call plus time to implement. Most members commit around 5–8 hours a week. Phase 3 is lighter with biweekly calls and async support as needed. The goal is to build a routine that's sustainable, not one that burns you out.

Do I need to already have clients to join?

You should have an offer and have sold it before. This program is built for people who have proof their work converts but need a system to make it consistent. If you're still figuring out your offer, The Strong Start is a better first step. Not sure where you fall? Reach out and we can talk it through.
